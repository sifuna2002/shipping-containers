/ Standard ISO Containers

ISO shipping containers such as 20' or 40' General Purpose are the most commonly used containers in shipping and road freight as they meet international standards. They are also built pallet wide accommodating 2 standard size pallets (side by side) and in high cube formats for an extra foot in height.

Other options may include cargo doors on one or both ends and additional side opening doors built in by the manufacturer or installed by our modifications team.
