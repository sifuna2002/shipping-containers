/ Portable Site Offices

Shipping containers are durable and modular in nature and as such provide the perfect solution for mobile site offices, temporary command centres or modular accommodation units.

All of our site offices are equipped with standard electrical fit outs, insulation, air conditioning, heavy duty vinyl flooring and personnel doors but can be modified further to suit your required work or living conditions. Site office containers can also be partitioned to include a lockable storage or work space area. We have 20ft, 40ft and 2x40ft site offices for sale and hire and can transport them to your site at your convenience.
