Small size shipping containers are typically 6', 8' or 10' but can be built to custom specs as required.

If you are unsure of your storage space requirements and it's a close call between a 10 and 20 foot container , it's usually better to opt for a the large size where possible to avoid over-stuffing the small container, it also works out cheaper per square foot of storage.
