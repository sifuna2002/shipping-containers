Perth Shipping Container Hire

SWISS PORT Containers has a large range of containers available to hire. Give us a call & our experienced sales team staff will assist to select the solution best for you. Hiring a container allows flexibility. You can hire any number, for just the amount of time required – short or long term and there is no need to worry about dealing with or selling a container when you no longer require it. You may also decide to “try before you buy” and purchase during the rental period.


They offer safe secure and vermin proof additional space at reasonable prices for a number of applications:


  • Storing furniture/household goods while renovating or moving
  • Storage for construction/building sites
  • General storage for business
  • Archive storage

Numerous sizes, grades & options are available:


  • 6’, 8’, 10’ small containers
  • 20’, 40’ general purpose or high cube
  • Special purpose containers including; refrigerated, dangerous goods, flat racks, bolsters, open tops, side opening
  • Offices

Transport can be arranged direct to you by tilt tray, side loader or HIAB, discuss with staff.
